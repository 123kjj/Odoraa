About Odoraa – Mom-Founded, Safe & Sustainable Odor Solutions

Odoraa is a mom-founded brand created by Devarshi Jain, built on a simple mission—to provide a safe, natural, and effective odor solution for modern families.

As a mother, Devarshi experienced the daily challenge of dealing with stubborn odors—from kitchen trash and cooking smells to diaper changes in the nursery. Most products she tried were either filled with harsh chemicals or only worked as temporary air fresheners that masked odors instead of eliminating them.

That’s when the idea for Odoraa was born.

Odoraa is designed as a solar powered odor absorber that works as a natural odor eliminator, helping remove unwanted smells at the source. It’s a non-toxic, reusable, and eco-friendly alternative to traditional air fresheners, making it ideal for homes with babies, kids, and pets.

Whether used as a:

  • Trash can odor remover
  • Kitchen odor eliminator
  • Diaper odor control solution in nurseries
  • Closet and shoe deodorizer

Odoraa is built to make everyday living cleaner, fresher, and healthier.

Our Mission

To create family-safe, sustainable home solutions that eliminate odors naturally without compromise.

Why Families Choose Odoraa

  • Non-toxic and safe for babies and pets
  • Solar-powered and reusable for long-term use
  • Eco-friendly alternative to chemical air fresheners
  • Designed by a mom who understands real home challenges
