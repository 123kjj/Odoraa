About Odoraa

A Mom-Founded Brand
Built for Real Families

Odoraa was created by Devarshi Jain — a mother on a mission to provide safe, natural, and effective odor solutions for modern homes.

The Story Behind Odoraa

As a mother, Devarshi experienced daily odor challenges from kitchens, trash cans, bathrooms, and more. Most products on the market only masked smells instead of truly eliminating them — and many contained chemicals she wasn't comfortable having around her family.

That frustration became the spark for Odoraa — a solar-powered odor absorber that removes odors at the source, using natural, non-toxic ingredients that are completely safe for kids and pets.

"Odoraa is not just a product; it's a solution created from real life, for real families." — Devarshi Jain, Founder
