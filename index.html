About Us 🌎

Odoraa is a mom-founded brand created by Devarshi Jain, built on a simple mission—to provide a safe, natural, and effective odor solution for modern families.

As a mother, she experienced daily challenges with odors from kitchens, trash cans, diapers, and more. Most products only masked smells instead of removing them.

That’s when Odoraa was created.

Odoraa is a solar powered odor absorber that removes odors at the source instead of covering them.

  • Trash can odor remover
  • Kitchen odor eliminator
  • Diaper odor control
  • Shoe and closet deodorizer

Our Mission: Create safe, sustainable home solutions that eliminate odors naturally.

Why families choose Odoraa:
